Background Valtaran Dwarf · Battle Master

Born in the Flintback Mountains, you spent much of your life working dangerous roads and wilderness routes between the mountain settlements and the settlements of the Shadowsong Peninsula.

Several years ago a rockslide trapped you and several companions in a remote canyon. Rescue seemed impossible.

Elowen Raincaller found you.

No one is entirely sure how.

Some say she followed a hawk spirit.

Others claim a mountain spirit guided her.

Whatever the truth, she led rescuers to your position and saved several lives. You have never forgotten it.

When word reaches Thunderford that Elowen has disappeared, you refuse to believe she simply wandered off. If she is in trouble, you intend to help. If someone harmed her, they will answer for it.
